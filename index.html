How can I track my shipments?

Create a shipment in the Open e-CMR portal and send the tracking code to the driver that will carry out the transport. He can easily enter the tracking code in the Open e-CMR app and keep you updated about your shipment in realtime.

How can I give real time updates to my transport clients?

Create transports in the Open e-CMR portal and assign them to your drivers. The driver gets a realtime notification about the new transport. All the information needed for the driver is available on his phone, such as the pickup & delivery location and all required transport documents. The driver notifies your clients about the status of the transport through the app.

Do I need to invest in new on-board computers?

Open e-CMR comes with a free app that can be downloaded in the Google Play store. Drivers simply install the Open e-CMR app on their Android compatible smartphone and are good to go. Once a transport is assigned to a driver it will automatically appear on their smartphone. The app supports the driver during the complete transport process, from the first pickup to the final delivery.
